Welcome to the Just For Tomorrow project.

We invite you to contribute to a future book loosely based on Narcotics Anonymous' "Just for Today" and Alcoholics Anonymous' "A Day at a Time"

Our Purpose

The purpose of the Just For Tomorrow project is to gather deliberately sarcastic daily readings (ie. affirmations, meditations, etc) that force you think "Just for Today". Our hope is that you will be creative and have fun with this.
